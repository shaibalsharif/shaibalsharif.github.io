About Bangladesh Institute of Planners (BIP)

What is BIP?

The Bangladesh Institute of Planners (BIP) is the national professional organization representing urban and regional planners in Bangladesh. Established to advance the planning profession and promote sustainable development, BIP serves as the authoritative voice for planners across the country.

Professional Excellence

BIP is dedicated to maintaining high standards of professional practice in urban and regional planning. The institute works to ensure that planning professionals adhere to ethical guidelines and best practices in their work.

Membership Community

BIP brings together planners from diverse backgrounds including government agencies, private consultancies, academic institutions, and development organizations to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing.

National Development

The institute plays a crucial role in shaping Bangladesh's urban and regional development policies, providing expert guidance on planning matters to government and other stakeholders.

Objectives of BIP

  • Advance the Planning Profession: Promote and advance the science and art of planning in all its aspects including economic, environmental, social, and physical planning at national, regional, and local levels
  • Professional Standards: Establish and maintain high standards of professional conduct, ethics, and competence among planners in Bangladesh
  • Education and Training: Facilitate continuing professional development through workshops, seminars, conferences, and training programs to enhance the skills and knowledge of planning professionals
  • Research and Innovation: Encourage and conduct research in planning theory, methodology, and practice to contribute to the body of planning knowledge
  • Policy Advocacy: Provide expert advice and recommendations to government, private sector, and civil society on planning and development matters affecting the nation
  • Public Awareness: Promote public understanding and appreciation of the planning profession and its contribution to sustainable development and quality of life
  • International Collaboration: Foster relationships with international planning organizations to exchange knowledge, experiences, and best practices
  • Member Services: Provide services and support to members including professional recognition, networking opportunities, and career development resources
  • Sustainable Development: Promote planning practices that support sustainable urban and regional development, environmental conservation, and climate resilience

BIP's Role in National Development

BIP contributes to Bangladesh's development agenda by:

  • Providing technical expertise to government planning initiatives
  • Reviewing and commenting on major development projects and policies
  • Advocating for evidence-based, sustainable planning approaches
  • Building capacity among planning professionals
  • Engaging with communities and stakeholders on planning issues

Why BIP Matters

In a rapidly urbanizing Bangladesh facing challenges of sustainable growth, climate change, and equitable development, BIP serves as a critical institution for:

  • Ensuring planned, orderly development of cities and regions
  • Protecting public interest in land use and development decisions
  • Promoting livable, resilient, and inclusive communities
  • Supporting professional planners in their important work
